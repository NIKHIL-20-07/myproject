Plan Your Journey

India changes with the sun and the rain. Here is your guide to the perfect timing.

❄️ Winter (Oct - March)
State City Why Now?
Rajasthan Jaipur / Udaipur Perfect weather for exploring palaces.
Kerala Munnar / Alleppey Cool breeze and lush green backwaters.
Tamil Nadu Madurai / Chennai Great for temple visits without the heat.
☀️ Summer (April - June)
State City Why Now?
Himachal Manali / Shimla Escape the heat in the snowy peaks.
Ladakh Leh High altitude passes open up for bikers.
Meghalaya Shillong / Cherrapunji Mist-covered hills and cooler temperatures.
🌧️ Monsoon (July - Sept)
State City Why Now?
Maharashtra Lonavala / Igatpuri Waterfalls and misty Western Ghats.
Goa South Goa Lush greenery and deep discounts.
Karnataka Coorg / Chikmagalur Experience the coffee plantations in the rain.
